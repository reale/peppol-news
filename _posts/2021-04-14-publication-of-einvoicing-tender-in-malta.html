--- layout: post title: Publication of eInvoicing Tender in Malta date: 2021-04-14 00:00:00 ---

 

The Government of Malta has launched a tender to establish an Agreement for the Provision of Peppol Networking and eInvoicing Services.

 

The closing date is 29th April 2021

 

Further details can be found at https://islands.einvoicing-project.eu/malta-launches-a-public-tender-to-procure-peppol-services/
