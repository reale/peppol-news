--- layout: post title: Second Call: European Multi-Stakeholder Forum on Electronic Invoicing (EMSFEI) date: 2018-02-19 00:00:00 ---

DG GROW has launched a call for members of the EMSFEI’s new mandate, which may be of interest to OpenPEPPOL members.

 

“The Commission is calling for applications with a view to selecting members of the Forum other than Member States’ authorities in charge of eInvoicing at government level, national fora for eInvoicing, where existing at national level, and other public entities.

 

This is the second call launched for the selection of members of the Forum. The first one was open until 15 September 2017. This call aims to select one organisation representing a common interest related to eInvoicing at national level, as referred to in chapter 2.1 (a), second indent, from each of the respective countries: Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Denmark, Greece, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia and Spain, as well as organisations at EU level representing different categories of stakeholders as referred to in chapter  2.1 (b).”

 

For more information please go to: https://ec.europa.eu/cefdigital/wiki/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=55892904

 

Deadline for submission is 6 March 2018.
