2020.03.03 List of Candidates_PUBLISHED

B. Call for Candidates extended for positions where not enough candidates exist

 

No candidacies were submitted for 4 open positions, listed further below.

 

The Election Committee decided on March 2nd, 2020 to extend the deadline for the submission of candidacies for the following positions, as described at:

2020.03.03 Extension to Call of Candidate_PUBLISHED

 

Positions to be elected by the Domain Communities

  1. eDelivery Community
    1. eDelivery Community Leader:
      • One (1) open position (eDECL) for a 2-year term
    2. eDelivery Change Management Board members:
      • Two (2) positions remain open
  2. Pre-Award Community
    1. Pre-Award Change Management Board members:
      • One (1) position remains open

 

The new deadline for submitting candidacies to the open positions above is Tuesday, March 10th, 2020.

Submission of candidacy, including a C.V. and motivation is to be sent to candidates@peppol.eu.

 

We strongly encourage all members to consider proposing candidates for the remaining open positions and contribute to the steering of activities that are vital for the success of ongoing work of the Association.

 

After all submissions have been received by March 10th, we will distribute the final list of candidates for all positions, and post their CVs and motivation in our members space on Confluence accordingly.

 

For any questions or clarifications, feel free to contact us at info@peppol.eu.
