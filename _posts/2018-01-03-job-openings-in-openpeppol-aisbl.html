--- layout: post title: Job openings in OpenPEPPOL AISBL date: 2018-01-03 00:00:00 ---

OpenPEPPOL has published 3 open positions for consultants interested in supporting the overarching effort in Connecting Europe through standardizing public procurement processes and Business Interoperability Specifications (PEPPOL BIS). OpenPEPPOL has assumed full responsibility for the development and maintenance of the PEPPOL specifications, building blocks and its services and implementation across Europe.

 

If you are an experienced and self-driven consultant interested in actively supporting  interoperability in Europe, OpenPEPPOL is the place to be. The association not only governs the most successful pan-European e-Procurement interoperability network, it also participates actively in increasing, expanding and maturing standardized Pan-European procurement processes.

 

The consultants are expected to start mid-January or as soon as possible thereafter, initially signing up for a 3-month trial period, with the possibility to be prolonged until April 1st, 2019, and then extended for a period of one year at the time.

 

Please go to the Assignments/Jobs page to read more about the vacancies.
