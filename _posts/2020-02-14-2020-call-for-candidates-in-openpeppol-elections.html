We hereby publish a ‘Call for Candidates’ for the following OpenPeppol governance positions open in 2020:

 

  1. Managing Committee:
    1. 1 representative of Peppol Authorities
    2. 1 representative of Service Providers
  2. eDelivery Community
    1. eDelivery Community Leader
    2. 3 members of the Change Management Board
  3. Post-Award Community
    1. 2 members of the Change Management Board
  4. Pre-Award Community
    1. Pre-Award Community Leader
    2. 2 members of the Change Management Board

 

You will find a detailed description of the open positions, the terms and conditions for eligibility of candidates and instructions about how to submit a candidacy at:

2020 Call for Candidates in OpenPeppol Elections_2020.02.14_v1.0

 

We strongly encourage all members who have both the interest and motivation, to consider putting forward candidates for any of the open positions.

 

For any questions or clarifications, feel free to contact us at info@peppol.eu.
