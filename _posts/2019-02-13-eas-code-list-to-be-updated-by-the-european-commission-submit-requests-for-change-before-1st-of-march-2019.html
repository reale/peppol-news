--- layout: post title: EAS Code list to be updated by the European Commission – Submit Requests for Change before 1st of March 2019 date: 2019-02-13 00:00:00 ---

 

On 15th of March 2019, the European Commission intends to publish version 2.0 of the Electronic Address Scheme (EAS) code list from which OpenPEPPOL is basing their participant identifiers.

 

Should you have the need to introduce a new code, please make sure to submit your request to the European Commission before 1st of March 2019.

 

Requests for Change for the addition of codes to the EAS can be submitted through the Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) Service Desk at: CEF Service Desk
