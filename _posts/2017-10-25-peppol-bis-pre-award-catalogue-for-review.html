--- layout: post title: PEPPOL BIS Pre-Award Catalogue for review date: 2017-10-25 00:00:00 ---

We are pleased to announce that after receiving the Pre-Award Coordinating Community’s  Change Management Board approval, the PEPPOL BIS Pre-Award Catalogue is now ready for review from OpenPEPPOL members .

 

The review period ends on the 20th of November, 2017..  Please find the link to the guide:  https://test-vefa.difi.no/peppolbis/pracc/catalogue/.

 

All issues that will be raised during the review period has to be registered in GitHub: https://github.com/OpenPEPPOL/pracc-catalogue/issues.  A validation support program for the PEPPOL BIS Pre-Award Catalogue is available at: https://test-vefa.difi.no/validator/.
