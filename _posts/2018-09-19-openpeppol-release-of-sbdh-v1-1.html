To: OpenPEPPOL eDelivery (TICC) and TICC-Dev Members,

 

This is the release of the SBDH v.1.1 which includes the possibility to include

  • document type identifier scheme and process identifier scheme
  • additional attributes

 

This version is backwards compatible with the previous version.

 

SBDH 1.1 is for immediate use in the PEPPOL network.

 

It is available via https://peppol.eu/downloads/the-peppol-edelivery-network-specifications

 

Or direct download through

https://github.com/OpenPEPPOL/documentation/blob/master/TransportInfrastructure/ICT-Transport-OpenPEPPOL-Envelope_Specification-11_2018-08-31.pdf.

 

On behalf of OpenPEPPOL eDelivery Change Management Board,
