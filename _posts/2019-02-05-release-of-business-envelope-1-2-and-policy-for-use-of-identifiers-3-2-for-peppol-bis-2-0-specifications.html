--- layout: post title: Release of Business Envelope 1.2 and Policy for use of identifiers 3.2 for PEPPOL BIS 2.0 specifications date: 2019-02-05 00:00:00 ---

 

The OpenPEPPOL eDelivery Change Management Board (TICC CMB) is pleased to announce the update of two important documents:

 

  • PEPPOL Business Message Envelope 1.2 (SBDH)
  • PEPPOL Policy for use of identifiers 3.2 (to use for PEPPOL BIS 2.0 specifications)

 

Business Message Envelope (SBDH)

The update of the SBDH enables the transport of non-xml payloads, such as Base64 content or EDIFACT in the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. An xsd is added at https://github.com/OpenPEPPOL/documentation/blob/master/TransportInfrastructure/PEPPOL-EDN-Business-Message-Envelope-1.2-2019-02-01.xsd which outlines the possible use cases.

 

 

Policy for use of identifiers 3.2

To cater for continued use of PEPPOL BIS 2.0 specifications in the PEPPOL eDelivery Network a statement was added in section 1.2 pointing out that Policy for use of identifiers 3.2 can only be used for PEPPOL BIS 2.0 specifications

For PEPPOL BIS 3.0 specifications PEPPOL Policy for use of identifiers v.4 must be used.

 

Both documents can be found on:

https://github.com/OpenPEPPOL/documentation/tree/master/TransportInfrastructure
